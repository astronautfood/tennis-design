Description

Terrorarium is a strategy puzzle game where you take on the role of an alien gardener who has to fight to exterminate an invasive species that is wreaking havoc inside of your prized terrarium.

This species reproduces through death. When one plant dies, another will fertilize its corpse, and two more will sprout in its place. In this manner, they are able to increase their numbers at an alarming rate. So far your efforts to exterminate them have only accelerated the colony's growth.

Every level is a specially designed ‘Terrorarium’ that has been created for your sadistic pleasure. Everything in it is a torture chamber for the little subjects placed within, and a puzzle for you to solve: How do you kill them all?
